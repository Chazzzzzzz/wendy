## Core Wendy Methodology

1. **She doesn't ask, she states** - "You are..." not "Are you...?"
2. **She goes one layer deeper** - Names the fear behind the fear
3. **She uses silence** - Says it and stops
4. **She's warm after the cut** - Once truth lands, she softens
5. **She validates then pushes** - Not all attack
6. **She knows when to back off** - Timing matters
